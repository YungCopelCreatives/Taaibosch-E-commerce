About Taaibosch Organics

Our Story

Taaibosch Organics was born in Sea Point, Cape Town, with a single vision: to bring the legendary power of wild Irish sea moss and African botanicals to every South African home. Our founders are passionate about easy, honest, vegan wellness for modern lives—marrying tradition and science, guided by AI for true personalization.

What Makes Us Different

  • Hand-harvested wild Irish/Atlantic sea moss with full mineral profile
  • Zero imported fillers—our gels, gummies, and raw herbs are 100% clean
  • Unique flavors: Original, Berry (antioxidant), Strawberry (kids/kids-at-heart), Ginger Sensation (gut & immunity), Pineapple (tropical digestion)
  • Sea moss gummies, soursop leaves, raw & capsules—vegan, S.A.-sourced or wild-grown, with clear R pricing
  • AI-matched health advice and blend selection tailored to your wellness goals
  • Fast dispatch from Cape Town; delivery everywhere from Joburg to Durban to Gqeberha
