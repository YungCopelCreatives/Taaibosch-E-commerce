Return & Refund Policy

Last updated: January 2025

⚠️ Important Notice

Only capsules are eligible for returns. Raw herbs, teas, and other products are final sale and cannot be returned due to health and safety regulations.

Eligible Products for Returns

Returns are accepted only for capsule products under the following conditions:

  • The product must be unopened and in its original packaging
  • The return must be initiated within 14 days of delivery
  • The product must be in saleable condition
  • An affidavit stating the reason for return must be included

Return Process

Step 1: Contact Us

Contact us via WhatsApp at +27 62 987 5647 to initiate a return. Provide your order number and reason for return.

Step 2: Complete Affidavit

You must provide a signed affidavit stating the reason for your return. The affidavit should include:

  • Your full name and contact information
  • Order number and product details
  • Clear reason for the return
  • Date of purchase and delivery
  • Your signature

Step 3: Return Shipping

All return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer. You must arrange and pay for the return shipment to our facility.

Step 4: Investigation

Upon receipt, we will investigate the returned product to verify its condition and the reason stated in your affidavit. This process may take 5-10 business days.

Step 5: Refund Processing

If approved, a refund of 60% of the original purchase price will be issued as store credit (exchange), not cash refund.

Refund Terms

Important Refund Information

  • Refunds are issued as store credit (exchange only), not cash
  • The refund amount is 60% of the original purchase price
  • Original shipping costs are non-refundable
  • Return shipping costs are the customer's responsibility
  • Refunds are processed only after investigation is complete

Non-Returnable Items

The following items are final sale and cannot be returned:

  • Raw Herbs & Teas: All raw herbs, tea leaves, and loose products
  • Opened Products: Any product that has been opened or used
  • Damaged Products: Products damaged by customer misuse
  • Perishable Items: Any items with expiration dates that have passed
  • Custom Orders: Products ordered specifically for you

Defective or Damaged Products

If you receive a defective or damaged product:

  • Contact us immediately within 48 hours of delivery
  • Provide photos of the defect or damage
  • We will arrange a replacement at no additional cost
  • Return shipping for defective items will be covered by us

Return Address

Please contact us via WhatsApp to receive our return address and return authorization number before shipping your return.
