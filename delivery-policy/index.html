Processing Time

All orders are processed and shipped within 1-2 business days (Monday-Friday, excluding public holidays) after payment confirmation or order placement via WhatsApp.

  • Orders placed before 2 PM on weekdays are typically shipped the same day
  • Orders placed after 2 PM or on weekends will be processed the next business day
  • During peak seasons, processing may take up to 3 business days
